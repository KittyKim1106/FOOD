1. 用户认证相关API
POST /api/auth/login
 用途: 用户登录
 请求字段:
   `username` (string): 用户名
   `password` (string): 密码
 响应字段:
   `success` (boolean)
   `userId` (string): 用户ID
   `token` (string): JWT token（用于后续请求认证）

POST /api/auth/register
 用途: 用户注册
 请求字段:
   `username` (string)
   `password` (string)
   `email` (string，可选)
 响应字段: 同login

2. 用户设置（排雷）相关API
GET /api/user/settings
 用途: 获取用户的排雷设置
 请求头: Authorization: Bearer {token}
 响应字段:
   `userId` (string)
   `excludedFlavors` (array): 排除的味道，如 ["Spicy", "Sour"]
   `excludedIngredients` (array): 排除的食材，如 ["Cilantro", "Garlic"]
   `lastUpdated` (timestamp)

PUT /api/user/settings
 用途: 更新用户的排雷设置
 请求头: Authorization: Bearer {token}
 请求字段:
   `excludedFlavors` (array)
   `excludedIngredients` (array)
 响应字段:
   `success` (boolean)
   `message` (string)

3. 决策逻辑相关API
POST /api/decision/recommend
 用途: 基于用户输入推荐食物
 请求头: Authorization: Bearer {token}
 请求字段:
   `intent` (string): "想吃" 或 "不想吃"
   `selectedCategories` (array): 选中的食物类别，如 ["Seafood", "Veggies"]
   `excludedFlavors` (array): 用户的排除味道（可从settings获取）
   `excludedIngredients` (array): 用户的排除食材
 响应字段:
   `recommendation` (object):
     `foodId` (string)
     `name` (string): 如 "红烧肉"
     `description` (string)
     `price` (number)
     `tags` (array): 如 ["Classic", "SaltySweet"]
     `imageUrl` (string)
   `confidence` (number): 匹配度，如 0.99

POST /api/decision/save
 用途: 保存用户的决策结果到历史
 请求头: Authorization: Bearer {token}
 请求字段:
   `foodId` (string)
   `decision` (string): "accepted" 或 "rejected"
   `timestamp` (timestamp)
 响应字段:
   `success` (boolean)

4. 历史记录相关API
GET /api/user/history
 用途: 获取用户的历史决策记录
 请求头: Authorization: Bearer {token}
 查询参数:
   `limit` (number, 默认10)
   `offset` (number, 默认0)
 响应字段:
   `history` (array of objects):
     `id` (string)
     `foodName` (string)
     `decision` (string)
     `timestamp` (timestamp)
     `isFavorite` (boolean)

PUT /api/user/history/{historyId}/favorite
 用途: 标记历史记录为收藏
 请求头: Authorization: Bearer {token}
 响应字段:
   `success` (boolean)
